Battalions

Hire/Fire Creatures

If a battalion already has creatures, any new creatures will come in with the same level and weapons as the rest of the battalion, which may increase cost.

In order to change creature type, you must first fire all the creatures in the battalion.

Train Battalion

Training a battalion increases its level, and therefore its attack and defense.

Arm Battalion

Arming your creatures can significantly multiply their attack! Most weapons require certain technologies or structures.
