Upgrades

Leader Level

Higher leader levels grant you attack and defense bonuses in combat, greater life points, and enable you to hire additional creature types. You will also gain more food from protecting more villagers, allowing you to hire more creatures into your army (creature points).

Technologies

Researching new technologies enables you to forge new weapons for your army and construct new structures.

Structures

Some structures enable other functions in the game, such as arming or training battalions. Other structures will increase your income.
